Passenger Rules

Baggage Rules

  • Carry-on baggage must not exceed 7kg.
  • Checked baggage allowance varies by airline.
  • Prohibited items include flammable substances, explosives, and sharp objects.

What You Can Carry

  • Medications with proper documentation.
  • Electronics in carry-on bags (laptops, cameras).
  • Liquids under 100ml in a transparent bag.

Lost Baggage

If your bag is lost:

  • Report to the airline's baggage service counter immediately.
  • Fill out a Property Irregularity Report (PIR).
  • Keep receipts for essential purchases for reimbursement.
